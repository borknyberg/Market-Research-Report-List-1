<p><p>The Military Land Inertial Navigation System (INS) market primarily includes two types: Single Antenna and Dual Antenna systems. Single Antenna INS utilizes one antenna to determine position and orientation, making it simpler and more cost-effective for various military applications. In contrast, Dual Antenna INS employs two antennas to enhance accuracy and provide better results in dynamic environments by measuring angular movement more effectively. Both systems are critical for improving navigation precision in military operations and optimizing strategy.</p></p>
