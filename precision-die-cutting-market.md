<p><p>Precision die cutting encompasses various techniques tailored for specific applications in manufacturing. Flat bed die cutting utilizes a flat platen to cut materials with high precision, ideal for thick or rigid substrates. Rotary die cutting employs a cylindrical die to produce continuous rolls, enhancing efficiency for high-volume production. Waterjet cutting utilizes high-pressure jets to cut through materials, allowing for intricate designs and versatility across various materials without heat distortion. Each method serves distinct needs in the precision die cutting market.</p></p>
