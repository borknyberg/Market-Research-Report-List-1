<p><p>Self-regulating heating cables are classified into three market types: CABT (Constant Attenuation Band Technology), CAMT (Constant Attenuation Modular Technology), and CAHT (Constant Attenuation High Temperature). CABT cables are designed for applications requiring consistent heating across various surfaces. CAMT focuses on modular designs for adaptability in installation, while CAHT is engineered to withstand high-temperature environments. Each type provides unique benefits, catering to specific heating needs in industrial, commercial, and residential applications, ensuring safety and energy efficiency.</p></p>
