<p><p>The sponge rubber materials market is categorized into three main types: closed-cell rubber, open-cell rubber, and other specialized forms. Closed-cell rubber consists of tiny, sealed pockets of gas, offering excellent insulation, buoyancy, and resistance to moisture, making it ideal for applications in automotive and construction. Open-cell rubber features interconnected cells, allowing for greater air permeability and flexibility, suitable for cushioning and sound absorption. Other market types may include specialized elastomers tailored for particular industrial uses, such as medical or aerospace applications.</p></p>
