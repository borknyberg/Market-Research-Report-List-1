<p><p>The polyurethane timing belt market features two primary types: trapezoidal tooth and arc tooth belts. Trapezoidal tooth polyurethane timing belts are characterized by their trapezoidal-shaped teeth, which offer efficient power transmission and reduced wear. In contrast, arc tooth polyurethane timing belts feature rounded teeth that provide smooth engagement and improved load distribution, minimizing noise and vibration. Both options are known for their durability and versatility, making them suitable for various industrial and automotive applications, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
