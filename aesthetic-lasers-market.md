<p><p>The aesthetic lasers market comprises three main types: gas lasers, solid lasers, and semiconductor lasers. Gas lasers, like CO2 lasers, are effective for skin resurfacing and tattoo removal due to their precision and depth control. Solid lasers, such as Nd:YAG, are versatile for various treatments, including hair removal and vascular lesions. Semiconductor lasers, often used in diode configurations, offer compact designs and efficiency, making them popular for hair removal and skin rejuvenation, with applications in diverse aesthetic procedures.</p></p>
