Rotary sputtering targets allow for continuous rotation during the sputtering process, enhancing uniform film deposition and improving target utilization efficiency. In contrast, non-rotatable targets are fixed in position, which might limit the uniformity of deposition but can be simpler and more cost-effective for certain applications. Both types serve critical roles in thin-film technology across various industries, including electronics and optics.</p></p>
