<p><strong>The Exogenous Creatinine Measurement Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jaffe's Kinetic Method</li><li>Enzymatic Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exogenous creatinine measurement kits primarily utilize two market types: the Jaffe's kinetic method and the enzymatic method. The Jaffe's method is based on the reaction of creatinine with picric acid, resulting in a color change that can be quantified spectrophotometrically. In contrast, the enzymatic method uses specific enzymes to convert creatinine into measurable products, offering higher specificity and sensitivity. Both methods are essential for assessing kidney function, with varying levels of accuracy and applicability in clinical settings.</p></p>
