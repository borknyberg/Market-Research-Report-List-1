<p><p>The heat-shrink sleeve labels market is characterized by various film types, each offering unique properties. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is popular for its affordability and versatility, while PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol) provides durability and clarity. OPS (Oriented Polystyrene) is lightweight and offers excellent printing quality. PE (Polyethylene) and PP (Polypropylene) films are known for their flexibility and resistance to moisture. COC (Cyclic Olefin Copolymer) films combine clarity with low-density features, making them suitable for specialized applications in packaging.</p></p>
