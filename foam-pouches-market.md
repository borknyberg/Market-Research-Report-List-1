<p><p>The foam pouches market consists of various polyethylene types, each catering to different packaging needs. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) offers strength and durability, making it suitable for heavy items. Middle Density Polyethylene (MDPE) provides a balance between flexibility and strength for moderate use. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is highly flexible and lightweight, ideal for delicate or lightweight products. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) combines the benefits of HDPE and LDPE, offering enhanced strength and puncture resistance, making it versatile for various applications.</p></p>
