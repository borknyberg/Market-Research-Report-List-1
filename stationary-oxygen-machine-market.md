<p><p>The stationary oxygen machine market comprises several types, including molecular sieve, macromolecule permeable membrane, electrolysis, and chemical methods. Molecular sieve technology utilizes adsorbent materials to separate oxygen from nitrogen in the air. Macromolecule permeable membranes selectively allow oxygen to pass through while blocking other gases. Electrolysis generates oxygen from water using an electrical current, while chemical methods involve reactions that produce oxygen. Other technologies may include emerging innovations and hybrid systems, catering to diverse applications in medical, industrial, and residential settings.</p></p>
