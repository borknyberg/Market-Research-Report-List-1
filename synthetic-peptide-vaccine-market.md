<p><strong>The Synthetic Peptide Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solution Phase Synthesis (SPS)</li><li>Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic peptide vaccine market comprises two primary types of synthesis methods: Solution Phase Synthesis (SPS) and Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS). SPS involves synthesizing peptides in a liquid solution, allowing for easy manipulation and purification of longer peptides. In contrast, SPPS attaches the peptide chain to a solid support, facilitating efficient stepwise synthesis and purification of shorter peptides. Both methods are pivotal in producing tailored vaccines for various diseases, enhancing immunogenic response, and enabling personalized medicine approaches.</p></p>
