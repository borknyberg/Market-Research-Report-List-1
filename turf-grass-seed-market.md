<p><ul><li>Cool Season Grass</li><li>Warm Season Grass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turf grass seed market is divided into two main types: cool season grass and warm season grass. Cool season grasses thrive in northern climates and grow best during cooler temperatures, offering lush green lawns in spring and fall. They include species like Kentucky bluegrass and fescue. In contrast, warm season grasses are suited for southern regions, flourishing in higher temperatures, and are typically dormant in cooler months. Examples include Bermuda and Zoysia grass, favored for their drought resistance and durability.</p></p>
