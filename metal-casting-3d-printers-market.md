<p><p>The metal casting 3D printers market includes several types, primarily Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM), along with other technologies. SLM uses high-powered lasers to fuse metal powder layer by layer, producing dense and complex parts. EBM, on the other hand, employs an electron beam in a vacuum, allowing for high thermal efficiency and less residual stress in parts. Other methods may involve binder jetting or direct energy deposition, expanding the range of applications in industries like aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
