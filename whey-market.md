<p><p>Whey market types include Acid Whey, Sweet Whey, Demineralized Whey, and Non-Demineralized Whey. Acid Whey, a byproduct of cheese production, has a lower protein content and is used in animal feed or fermentation. Sweet Whey, derived from rennet cheese, is rich in protein and used in health products and dietary supplements. Demineralized Whey undergoes processing to reduce mineral content, enhancing its nutritional profile for specialized applications. Non-Demineralized Whey retains its natural mineral content and is often used in food and beverage formulations.</p></p>
