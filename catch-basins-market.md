<p><ul><li>Drop Inlets Catch Basins</li><li>Curb Opening Catch Basins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The catch basins market comprises two main types: drop inlet catch basins and curb opening catch basins. Drop inlet catch basins are designed to capture surface water directly from the ground, utilizing vertical openings for efficient drainage. On the other hand, curb opening catch basins are integrated into street curbs, allowing water to flow in from the road surface. Both types function to prevent flooding and manage stormwater runoff, playing a crucial role in urban drainage systems.</p></p>
