<p><p>The CVD epitaxy equipment market is primarily divided into two types: MOCVD (Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment and other CVD epitaxy equipment. MOCVD is widely used for growing semiconductor layers, particularly in optoelectronics and high-frequency applications, due to its ability to produce high-quality materials. Other CVD equipment encompasses various techniques like APCVD (Atmospheric Pressure CVD) and LPCVD (Low-Pressure CVD), catering to diverse applications including solar cells, microelectronics, and advanced materials in numerous industries.</p></p>
