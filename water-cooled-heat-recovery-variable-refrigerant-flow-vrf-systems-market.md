<p><ul><li>2 Pipe Systems</li><li>3 Pipe Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water-cooled Heat Recovery Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) systems can be categorized into two main types: 2-pipe and 3-pipe systems. </p><p>2-pipe systems utilize one refrigerant circuit for both heating and cooling, switching fluid flow direction based on demand, making them efficient but limited to simultaneous heating and cooling. </p><p>In contrast, 3-pipe systems feature separate pipelines for heating and cooling, allowing for simultaneous temperature control in different zones, enhancing comfort and efficiency in commercial and large residential applications.</p></p>
