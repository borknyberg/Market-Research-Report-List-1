<p><p>The acrylic lenses market primarily comprises two types: monofocal intraocular lenses (IOLs) and premium IOLs. Monofocal IOLs are designed to provide clear vision at a single distance, typically used for patients undergoing cataract surgery. In contrast, premium IOLs include multifocal, accommodating, and toric lenses, offering advanced vision correction for multiple distances and addressing astigmatism. The premium category appeals to patients seeking enhanced visual outcomes and greater freedom from glasses, thus driving innovation and growth within the acrylic lenses market.</p></p>
