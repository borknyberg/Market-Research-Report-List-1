<p><p>The medical resin market includes Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) and High Density Polyethylene (HDPE), which are both widely used in healthcare applications. LDPE is valued for its flexibility, chemical resistance, and ease of processing, making it suitable for soft packaging and medical bags. Conversely, HDPE offers superior strength and durability, ideal for rigid containers and pharmaceutical packaging. Both materials are essential for manufacturing products such as syringes, vials, and medical devices, meeting the stringent requirements of the medical industry.</p></p>
