<p><p>The gelatin-based plasma expander market is categorized into three primary types based on gelatin content: 4.0%, 3.5%, and 3.2%. The 4.0% content is often used for more significant fluid volume expansion and is commonly employed in critical care settings. The 3.5% content offers a balanced approach, providing adequate expansion while minimizing side effects. The 3.2% content is typically used in less severe cases, helping to maintain intravascular volume with lower osmotic pressure. Each type serves specific clinical applications.</p></p>
