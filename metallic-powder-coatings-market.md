<p><strong>The Metallic Powder Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonding Process</li><li>Blending Process</li><li>Extrusion Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic powder coatings market consists of three main processes: bonding, blending, and extrusion. In the bonding process, metallic powders are fused onto substrates to create a durable finish. The blending process involves mixing metallic powders with resins to achieve desired aesthetics and properties before application. The extrusion process produces powder pellets by forcing a mixture through a die, enabling efficient manufacturing of coatings. Each method contributes to the versatility and performance of coatings used in various applications.</p></p>
