<p><p>The benchtop oscilloscopes market features primarily two types: two-channel models and four-channel models. Two-channel oscilloscopes are designed for basic signal analysis, allowing users to observe two signals simultaneously, making them ideal for simple applications. In contrast, four-channel models enable more complex analysis, accommodating four input signals, which is beneficial for advanced research and development tasks. The choice between the two often depends on the specific requirements of the user, including the complexity of the signals being tested.</p></p>
