<p><strong>The Polyurethane Spray Foam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Closed Cell Injection Foam</li><li>Open Cell Spray Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane spray foam market is primarily divided into two types: rigid closed cell injection foam and open cell spray foam. Rigid closed cell foam is characterized by its dense structure, providing superior insulation, moisture resistance, and structural support, making it ideal for roofing and foundation applications. In contrast, open cell spray foam is lighter and more flexible, offering excellent sound absorption and thermal insulation, typically used in interior applications like walls and attics for improved energy efficiency.</p></p>
