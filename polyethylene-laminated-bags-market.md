Light duty bags are suitable for lightweight items and short-term storage, often used in retail. Medium duty bags provide greater durability for heavier products and are commonly used in shipping and storage applications. Heavy duty bags are designed for maximum strength, capable of holding substantial weight, making them ideal for industrial purposes and bulk packaging. Each type serves specific needs in the packaging market.</p></p>
