<p><strong>The Cellulose-based Nonwoven Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunlace Methods</li><li>Needle-punched Methods</li><li>Meltblowing Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellulose-based nonwoven materials market includes various manufacturing methods like spunlace, needle-punched, and meltblowing. Spunlace involves high-pressure jets of water to entangle fibers, producing soft, absorbent fabrics ideal for hygiene products. Needle-punched techniques use barbed needles to mechanically bond fibers, resulting in durable materials suitable for industrial applications. Meltblowing, on the other hand, extrudes molten polymer through small nozzles to create fine fibers, yielding lightweight, high-filtration media. Each method caters to specific performance and application needs.</p></p>
